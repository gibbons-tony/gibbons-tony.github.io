# Blog Post Image Generation Prompts

## Design System Guidelines

**Style:** Material Design vector graphics with vibrant gradients
**Color Palette:** Google Material Design colors with emphasis on blues (#4285F4), greens (#34A853), yellows (#FBBC04), and reds (#EA4335)
**Aesthetic:** Clean, geometric, minimalist with subtle depth through shadows and gradients
**Composition:** Bold, simple shapes with clear focal points
**Mood:** Professional yet playful, innovative, forward-thinking

## Additional Generation Tips

### For Best Results:
1. Add to prompt: "Vector illustration, Material Design style, flat design with subtle shadows"
2. Specify: "No photorealistic elements, no complex textures, geometric shapes only"
3. Include: "Clean lines, bold colors, minimalist composition"
4. Consider: "White or light background for versatility"
5. Request: "High contrast for web viewing"

### Size Specifications:
- **Thumbnails:** 16:9 ratio, optimize for 800x450px minimum
- **Article Images:** Square or 16:9, optimize for 1200px width
- **File format:** Request PNG with transparency or SVG if available

### Material Design Color Codes:
- **Blue:** #4285F4 (Google Blue)
- **Green:** #34A853 (Google Green)
- **Yellow:** #FBBC04 (Google Yellow)
- **Red:** #EA4335 (Google Red)
- **Deep Blue:** #1A73E8
- **Light Blue:** #E8F0FE
- **Purple:** #9C27B0
- **Orange:** #FF9800

### Avoid:
- Photorealistic elements
- Complex gradients beyond 2-3 colors
- Overly detailed backgrounds
- Small text that won't scale
- Cultural symbols that may not translate globally
