A practical guide to IoT implementation from someone who's been in the trenches. Kranz, as a Cisco executive, brings real-world experience to what could easily become a theoretical discussion.

The book excels at bridging the gap between technical possibilities and business realities. Rather than getting lost in the technology, it focuses on practical implementation strategies, common pitfalls, and lessons learned from actual IoT deployments.

Particularly valuable are the case studies and the framework for thinking about IoT projects - from pilot to production scale. The emphasis on starting small, proving value, and then scaling is a refreshing contrast to the "transform everything" approach many vendors push.

Essential reading for anyone involved in IoT strategy or implementation, whether on the technical or business side.
