## The Challenge: Data Center R&D's Massive Spend with Little Control

<div class="pull-quote float-right">
"We transformed massive Data Center R&D spend from black box to transparent portfolio with unprecedented visibility"
</div>

{% include figure.liquid path="assets/img/casestudies/time_tracking/iter-001-image-light-2026-03-11T17-58-52.jpg" class="img-fluid rounded z-depth-1" style="max-width: 45%; float: right; margin: 0 0 20px 20px;" zoomable=true %}

Working in **Data Center R&D with leaders Bart Sano and Nan Boden**, I confronted a critical challenge: **massive spend with little control or visibility**. The organization was investing billions in R&D, but lacked any systematic way to understand what teams were actually working on or how those investments translated to business value.

**The reality was stark:**
- Billions in R&D spend with no portfolio visibility
- No connection between work and profitability
- Annual planning based on guesswork and politics
- Engineers spent 4+ hours weekly on manual status updates
- Executives made billion-dollar decisions blind

The fundamental questions—"What is our R&D portfolio actually building?" and "Which investments are profitable?"—couldn't be answered with data. This wasn't just inefficiency; it was strategic blindness at scale.

## Our Innovation: Gamifying R&D Portfolio Visibility

{% include figure.liquid path="assets/img/casestudies/time_tracking/iter-001-image-light-2026-03-11T18-00-55.jpg" class="img-fluid rounded z-depth-1" style="max-width: 50%; float: left; margin: 0 20px 20px 0;" zoomable=true %}

Embodying the "zero-to-one problem solving" approach that excites me, **my team and I created a nimble, fun method of capturing and gamifying "what people are working on"**—transforming a painful compliance task into an engaging system that provided unprecedented visibility into the R&D portfolio and its profitability.

**1. We Innovated on Process: Making It Fun and Nimble**

Instead of another burdensome tracking system, we gamified the experience:
- **Made it fun:** Turned tracking into a lightweight, engaging activity
- **Nimble approach:** Quick updates, not lengthy forms
- **Focus on value:** Connected work to business impact, not just hours
- **R&D portfolio view:** Showed how individual work contributed to larger initiatives
- **Profitability lens:** Linked activities to revenue and cost centers

**2. My Team and I Built the Application**

**We drove the engineering**—this wasn't outsourced or handed off:
- I personally architected the technical solution
- My team and I coded the full-stack web application
- Built real-time dashboards showing R&D portfolio composition
- Created profitability analytics connecting work to business value
- Integrated with existing Data Center systems for seamless data flow

**3. Political Navigation and Trust Building**

The technical solution was only half the battle—we had to win hearts and minds:
- **Built trust with Bart Sano and Nan Boden:** Demonstrated value with pilot data
- **Converted skeptics:** Showed how visibility helped protect R&D budgets
- **Made allies of finance:** Gave them the data they desperately needed
- **Protected engineers:** Ensured the tool helped rather than burdened them

**4. Strategic Partnership with PMO for Company-Wide Rollout**

{% include figure.liquid path="assets/img/casestudies/time_tracking/iter-001-image-light-2026-03-11T18-02-43.jpg" class="img-fluid rounded z-depth-1" zoomable=true %}
<div class="caption">
From Data Center R&D innovation to company-wide platform: Our success led to PMO partnership for scale
</div>

After proving value in Data Center R&D, **we partnered with PMO** to scale company-wide:
- **Our innovation drove adoption:** Success in R&D created demand elsewhere
- **PMO as scaling partner:** They helped with organizational rollout, not engineering
- **Maintained ownership:** My team continued to evolve the product
- **From 10 to 10,000+:** Scaled from pilot to company-wide in under a year

**5. Executive Greenlight from Sundar Pichai**

The strategic value of our R&D visibility innovation was recognized at the highest level—**Sundar Pichai himself greenlit the initiative**, validating our approach of making the invisible visible.

## The Outcome: From R&D Visibility to Billion-Dollar P&L Foundation

Our innovation transformed Data Center R&D from a black box of massive spend into a transparent, profitable portfolio—then scaled to become critical company infrastructure:

**Immediate Impact in Data Center R&D:**
- **Unprecedented visibility**: First-ever clear view of R&D portfolio composition
- **Profitability insights**: Connected R&D investments to business value
- **Massive spend under control**: Billions in spend now had accountability
- **Bart Sano and Nan Boden's endorsement**: Leadership champions for broader rollout
- **Cultural shift**: From compliance burden to engaging visibility tool

**Company-Wide Transformation:**
- **10,000+ users**: Scaled from Data Center R&D to entire organization
- **Annual planning revolution**: Data replaced guesswork in billion-dollar decisions
- **Real-time portfolio management**: Leaders could see and adjust investments dynamically
- **Engineering protection**: Tool helped justify and protect R&D budgets with data

**Long-Term Value (The Full-Circle Story):**

{% include figure.liquid path="assets/img/casestudies/time_tracking/iter-001-image-light-2026-03-11T18-04-24.jpg" class="img-fluid rounded z-depth-1" style="max-width: 50%; float: right; margin: 0 0 20px 20px;" zoomable=true %}

Years later, when I was building the [Cost & Profitability Framework](/casestudies/profitability_framework/), I discovered our R&D visibility system was **still in use**—its functionality had been integrated into the official 'moma' suite.

More remarkably, **our Data Center R&D innovation became the foundation for activity-based payroll allocation** in a multi-billion dollar P&L framework. The gamified "what are you working on" data we captured enabled unprecedented accuracy in allocating engineering costs—one of the largest expense categories in the business.

**The profitability lens we created in Data Center R&D became the blueprint for company-wide financial analytics.**

**Why This Matters:**

This project proves that massive organizational challenges (billions in uncontrolled spend) can be solved with innovative thinking (gamification), technical excellence (we built it ourselves), and political savvy (converting skeptics to champions).

What started as addressing Data Center R&D's visibility crisis evolved into critical infrastructure that still drives billion-dollar decisions today—showing how foundational work compounds over time when you build it right from the start.

**Core Innovations:**
- **Gamification of compliance**: Made tracking fun instead of painful
- **Portfolio visibility framework**: Connected individual work to strategic initiatives
- **Profitability analytics**: Linked R&D work to business value
- **Scalable architecture**: Built for Data Center, scaled company-wide
- **Political navigation**: From Bart Sano's support to Sundar's greenlight

The project showcased that even early in career, with the right approach to innovation and execution, you can build systems that transform how trillion-dollar companies operate.
